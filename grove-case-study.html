Creative Work

01

Grove — Editorial

Single Ingredient Hero.
Campaign Ad

Editorial CompositeStatic AdMeta Feed 1080×1350

Warm parchment background. A single ingredient centred with extreme negative space. The entire brief was "the ingredient is the hero" — so there is nothing else. No graphic system, no benefit callouts, no lifestyle overlay. Photography-forward with colour grading that leans warm and honest rather than clean and aspirational. Photoshop.

02

Grove — Motion

Ingredient Reveal.
Brand Film

After EffectsBrand FilmSquare 1080×1080

Organic reveal of the hero ingredient — slow, textural, unhurried. The pacing is the opposite of fast fashion motion work: natural material, natural tempo. Warm tones throughout, no artificial sharpness. The motion is designed to feel like watching something grow rather than watching something load. After Effects.

Brand Identity

Creative Direction

Diaspora Co., Brightland, Burlap & Barrel. The ingredient as hero, provenance as brand story. No graphic system forced on top of the photography — the food earns the frame on its own terms. Warmth without sentimentality.

Typography & Tone

Serif with warmth — not decorative, just honest. Parchment tone, forest green accents. Copy is conversational and specific: the name of the ingredient, where it came from, nothing more. Natural without being folksy.

Colour & Atmosphere

Warm parchment #f4ede2 — Deep forest green #1a2e1b — Natural linen. Warmth without artificiality. The palette should feel like a farmers market at 8am, not a wellness brand at 8pm.
