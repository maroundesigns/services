The Brief

Premium organic
without the preachiness.

GROVE is a premium organic food brand with a problem most sustainable brands share — the creative looks as worthy as the product, but not as desirable. Health food ads typically lead with claims. GROVE needed to lead with feeling.

The brief: make organic food look like something you want, not something you should have. Warm, earthy, premium — without a single green juice cliche in sight.

Just Grove — Brand Ad

The Strategy

Show the ingredient.
Tell the story.

The two-piece strategy puts emotion first and product second. The motion piece creates atmosphere through close-up ingredient cinematography. The static ad anchors that atmosphere with a clean branded moment built for feed placement.

Stage 01

Feel

The ingredient reveal motion piece runs top-of-funnel. Words dissolve over close-up ingredient footage. No product, no claims — just the world GROVE lives in. Earthy, slow, confident.

Stage 02

Choose

Creative Decisions

Warmth as
positioning.

The forest green and terracotta palette was chosen to feel like the land the ingredients come from — not the brand guidelines of a supplement company. It positions GROVE as a food brand with taste, not a wellness brand with claims.

Slow pacing in the motion piece was intentional. Fast cuts feel like urgency. Slow cuts feel like confidence. GROVE doesn’t need to sell hard — it needs you to want in.
