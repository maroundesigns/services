Brand Identity

Creative Direction

Dark alternative feminine energy. Urban Decay, Juliette Has a Gun, Vivienne Westwood adjacency. Fashion-forward, not mainstream. Dark without being Gothic. The woman who wears ROUGE is not trying to be liked.

Typography & Tone

Bodoni in full caps at 400–600 tracking. White at 85–90% opacity. Copy is declarative, not descriptive — "Wear the Dark." is a command, not a tagline. No softness, no warmth in the language.

Colour & Atmosphere

Deep crimson #8B0000 — Near black #080808 — Silver hardware accents. Dangerous red, not romantic red. The palette should feel like a bruise at midnight, not a valentine.
