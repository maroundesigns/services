Halo — Editorial

Lifestyle Ad.
Brand Editorial

Lifestyle editorial for a sleep app built entirely around atmosphere rather than product features. Low-light environment, the phone as a soft glow — present but not pushed. The creative brief was simple: make rest feel like a state worth entering, not a problem to be solved. Deep navy tones, minimal copy. Photoshop.

Halo — Motion

Animated Social Ad.
Stories Format

After EffectsMotion GraphicsStories 1080×1920

Gentle, rhythmic animation timed to a sleep-state pace — each element expanding and contracting like a slow breath. Breathwork metaphors embedded in the motion itself, not described in copy. Deep navy tones with soft luminance gradients. Built for Stories and Reels placement where the first second has to earn the next. After Effects.

Brand Identity

Creative Direction

Calm and Headspace as reference points, but pushed toward more world-building and less habit-tracking. Sleep as ritual rather than optimisation. The creative should feel like an invitation, not an instruction.

Typography & Tone

Light weight serif, lowercase, generous leading. Whisper-tone, not broadcast-tone. Copy is suggestive and experiential — it describes a feeling rather than a feature. The pace of the words matches the pace of the motion.

Colour & Atmosphere

Deep navy #080c1e — Soft indigo — Pale luminance glow. The colour of the hour before sleep. Nothing bright, nothing sharp. The palette should lower your cortisol, not raise it.
